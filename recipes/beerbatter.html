Beer batter Recipe

Description

This beer batter recipe is the key to fried white fish thatâ€™s crispy and golden on the outside, but tender on the inside. You can also use it to perfectly fry veggies, shrimp, and so much more!

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cornstarch
  • egg
  • Seasonings: Garlic powder, black pepper, and seasoning salt.
  • Beer, light or dark is fine.

    • Steps

    1. Combine flour, cornstarch, egg, garlic powder, seasoning salt, and black pepper in a medium bowl. Whisk in beer until smooth and thick, similar to pancake batter.
